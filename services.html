  • Massage therapy benefits people of all ages. While it benefits the injured, the ill and the stressed, the strength of massage therapy in preventing illness and conditions before they develop and cannot be overlooked. Massage therapy can be used in the treatment of both acute and chronic stages of conditions.

    Therapeutic massage is an important part of your health maintenance plan, by:

    • Reducing or eliminating pain
    • Improving joint mobility
    • Improving circulation
    • Improving immune system functioning
    • Increasing lymphatic drainage
    • Reducing depression and anxiety
    • Reducing tension within muscles
    • Increasing body awareness
